some zines

Most of these zines, with only one exception, were created in ways which allow for on-screen reading and/or DIY print and assemble. The Situated Knowledges zines were distributed as physical copies during the Situated Systems residency at Pier 9. For each of the other zines, I created a limited amount of printed, hand bound copies which I distributed as gifts.
